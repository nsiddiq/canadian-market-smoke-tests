About Us

Local Experts Who Care About Your Property

Pen Home Watch was founded in 2019 by Sarah and David Mitchell, lifelong South Okanagan residents who saw a growing need for reliable, professional home monitoring in the Penticton and Summerland area.

After years in property management, they built Pen Home Watch on a simple principle: treat every client's home as if it were their own. Today, their team of certified home watch professionals serves over 200 properties across the South Okanagan.

As accredited members of the National Home Watch Association (NHWA), we adhere to the highest industry standards for property monitoring, reporting, and client communication.
