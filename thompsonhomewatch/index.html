What We Do

Built for Thompson Valley's Climate

Kamloops and area has unique challenges — extreme cold, wildfire risk, and dramatic temperature swings. Our services are designed specifically for this region.

Full Interior & Exterior Checks

Plumbing/heating verification, security/lighting checks, insurance-compliant logs

Comprehensive scheduled walkthroughs of your entire property, inside and out. We verify every system is functioning correctly and document everything for your records and insurance requirements.

  • ✓ HVAC system operation & thermostat verification
  • ✓ Plumbing inspection — faucets, toilets, water heater, visible pipes
  • ✓ Security system testing & entry point checks
  • ✓ Timed lighting & occupancy simulation
  • ✓ Timestamped photo reports after every visit
  • ✓ Full insurance-compliant documentation logs

Wildfire & Storm Readiness

Defensible space, vent checks, evacuation response

Thompson Valley faces serious wildfire risk every summer. Our FireSmart-trained team proactively monitors your property and responds to evacuation alerts on your behalf.

  • ✓ Defensible space clearance & vegetation management
  • ✓ Ember-resistant vent and roof inspection
  • ✓ Gutter and debris clearance
  • ✓ Evacuation alert monitoring & rapid response
  • ✓ Post-storm damage assessment & documentation
  • ✓ Insurance-ready photo evidence collection

Winter Failure Protection

Frozen pipes, snow loads, 24/7 heating monitoring

Kamloops winters regularly hit -30°C. Unmonitored homes are vulnerable to catastrophic pipe bursts, ice dams, and heating failures. We catch problems before they become disasters.

  • ✓ Pipe freeze prevention — thermostat & heat trace monitoring
  • ✓ Ice dam detection on rooflines and gutters
  • ✓ Roof snow load assessment after major storms
  • ✓ Furnace operation verification every visit
  • ✓ 24/7 heating failure alert response
  • ✓ Full winterization & spring opening services

Keyholder & Contractor Access

Supervised entry, mail/flyer removal, pre-arrival setup

We act as your trusted local representative. Need a plumber let in? Groceries stocked before you arrive? We handle it all so your home is ready when you are.

  • ✓ Supervised contractor & tradesperson entry
  • ✓ Mail, package & flyer collection
  • ✓ Pre-arrival home preparation & heating startup
  • ✓ Grocery delivery & restocking coordination
  • ✓ Utility & vendor liaison on your behalf
  • ✓ Vehicle battery & startup checks

24/7 Emergency Dispatch

On-site assessment, photo docs for insurance, trade coordination

When something goes wrong, every minute counts. Our team is local and ready to respond immediately — day or night — to protect your property from further damage.

  • ✓ Immediate local on-site assessment
  • ✓ Full photo & video documentation for insurance claims
  • ✓ Emergency water shutoff & damage mitigation
  • ✓ Trusted local trade coordination for repairs
  • ✓ Direct communication with you & your insurance
  • ✓ Follow-up monitoring until issue is resolved

Specialized Resort Care

Ski/chalet monitoring, snow removal, seasonal opening/closing

Sun Peaks and area recreational properties need specialized care. We understand resort living — from heavy snow loads to extended vacancy between ski trips.

  • ✓ Ski property & chalet monitoring between visits
  • ✓ Snow removal & driveway clearing coordination
  • ✓ Seasonal opening setup — heat, water, supplies
  • ✓ End-of-season closing & winterization
  • ✓ Hot tub & outdoor equipment checks
  • ✓ Guest-ready preparation for rental properties

About Us

Thompson Valley Locals Protecting Your Investment

Thompson Home Watch was founded in 2020 by Mike and Karen Aldridge, a husband-and-wife team who spent over 15 years in Kamloops real estate before recognizing an urgent gap in the local market.

With Kamloops growing as a retirement and recreational destination — and Sun Peaks attracting out-of-province property owners — they saw hundreds of homes sitting unmonitored through dangerous wildfire seasons and -30°C winter stretches.

Today, Thompson Home Watch monitors over 150 properties from downtown Kamloops to the slopes of Sun Peaks. Their team is trained in FireSmart protocols and holds certifications from the National Home Watch Association.
