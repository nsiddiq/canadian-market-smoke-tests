What We Do

Built for Thompson Valley's Climate

Kamloops has unique challenges — extreme cold, wildfire risk, and dramatic temperature swings. Our services are designed specifically for this region.

Home Watch Inspections

Complete interior and exterior inspections with heating system verification, plumbing checks, and security system testing.

Wildfire Season Readiness

FireSmart compliance checks, defensible space maintenance, ember-resistant vent monitoring, and evacuation alert response.

Winter Protection

Frozen pipe prevention, ice dam monitoring, snow load assessment, heating failure alerts, and winterization services.

Concierge & Key Holder

Contractor access, mail management, pre-arrival preparation, and utility coordination when you're away.

Emergency Response

24/7 emergency availability with rapid on-site assessment, insurance documentation, and emergency repair coordination.

Sun Peaks Property Care

Specialized ski property management including snow removal coordination, access checks, and seasonal opening/closing.

About Us

Thompson Valley Locals Protecting Your Investment

Thompson Home Watch was founded in 2020 by Mike and Karen Aldridge, a husband-and-wife team who spent over 15 years in Kamloops real estate before recognizing an urgent gap in the local market.

With Kamloops growing as a retirement and recreational destination — and Sun Peaks attracting out-of-province property owners — they saw hundreds of homes sitting unmonitored through dangerous wildfire seasons and -30°C winter stretches.

Today, Thompson Home Watch monitors over 150 properties from downtown Kamloops to the slopes of Sun Peaks. Their team is trained in FireSmart protocols and holds certifications from the National Home Watch Association.
