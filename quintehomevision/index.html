About Us

Quinte Region Locals Who Know Your Property Needs

Quinte Home Vision was established in 2018 by James and Rachel Connors, born-and-raised Quinte Region residents with deep roots in the community. After 20 years in facility management and property maintenance, they launched Quinte Home Vision to serve the growing number of seasonal and absentee property owners in the area.

With Prince Edward County's boom as a tourist destination and Belleville's growth attracting out-of-town investors, the need for professional, reliable property monitoring has never been greater. Their team of certified home watch professionals now serves over 175 properties across the region.

As proud members of the National Home Watch Association and active participants in the Quinte & District Real Estate Board community, they bring credibility, local knowledge, and genuine care to every client relationship.
